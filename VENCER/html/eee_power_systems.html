Power Systems Challenge

Master the Art of Power System Design and Analysis

Event Description

The Power Systems Challenge invites participants to demonstrate their expertise in power system design, analysis, and optimization. Participants will work on real-world power system scenarios, implement advanced control strategies, and create solutions that showcase both technical proficiency and practical applicability. This competition is perfect for those passionate about power systems engineering and sustainable energy solutions.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Individual or team participation (max 3 members)
  • Duration: 6 hours
  • Use of standard power system simulation tools allowed
  • Original solution implementation required
  • System efficiency and stability will be evaluated
  • Technical documentation mandatory
  • Final presentation of solution required
  • Implementation feasibility must be demonstrated

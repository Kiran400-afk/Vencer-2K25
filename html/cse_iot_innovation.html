IoT Innovation Challenge

Event Description

Showcase your IoT development skills by creating innovative solutions for real-world problems. This competition challenges participants to build smart, connected devices and systems.

Rules

  • Teams of 2-4 members are allowed
  • Duration: 12 hours
  • Participants must bring their own hardware components
  • Basic sensors and microcontrollers will be provided
  • Projects must be original and created during the event
  • Cloud connectivity is mandatory

Requirements

  • Knowledge of IoT protocols (MQTT, CoAP)
  • Experience with microcontrollers (Arduino/ESP32)
  • Basic electronics understanding
  • Cloud platform familiarity (AWS IoT, Azure IoT)

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation and practicality (35%)
  • Technical implementation (25%)
  • Connectivity and data handling (20%)
  • Project presentation (20%)

Prize Pool

  • 1st Place: ₹30,000
  • 2nd Place: ₹20,000
  • 3rd Place: ₹10,000

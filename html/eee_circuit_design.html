Circuit Design Competition

Innovate through electronic circuit design

Event Description

The Circuit Design Competition challenges participants to create innovative electronic circuits that solve real-world problems. From analog to digital circuits, showcase your expertise in circuit design, simulation, and implementation. Push the boundaries of electronic engineering with your creative solutions!

Rules & Guidelines

  • Individual or team participation (max 2 members)
  • Duration: 8 hours
  • Design and simulate your circuit using industry-standard tools
  • Software allowed: Proteus, Multisim, or LTspice
  • Must include circuit simulation and analysis
  • Designs must be original and energy-efficient
  • Include component list and cost analysis
  • Present working prototype or detailed simulation

VLSI Design Challenge

Innovate in Integrated Circuit Design

Event Description

The VLSI Design Challenge invites participants to showcase their expertise in designing cutting-edge integrated circuits. Participants will work on complex circuit designs, implement advanced VLSI techniques, and create innovative solutions that demonstrate both technical proficiency and practical applicability. This competition is perfect for those passionate about pushing the boundaries of semiconductor technology and integrated circuit design.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Individual or team participation (max 3 members)
  • Duration: 48 hours
  • Industry-standard EDA tools allowed
  • Original design implementation required
  • Power, area, and timing constraints must be met
  • Complete documentation mandatory
  • Final presentation and design review required
  • Design verification strategy must be included

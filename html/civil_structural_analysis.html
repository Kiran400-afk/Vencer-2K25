Structural Analysis Contest

Master the art of structural engineering

Event Description

The Structural Analysis Contest challenges participants to analyze complex structural systems and optimize their performance using modern engineering tools. From high-rise buildings to complex industrial structures, participants will demonstrate their expertise in structural mechanics, load analysis, and design optimization. This competition emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team participation (2-3 members)
  • Duration: 10 hours
  • Use of structural analysis software
  • Load calculation and analysis
  • Seismic analysis required
  • Material optimization
  • Cost efficiency analysis
  • Final presentation mandatory

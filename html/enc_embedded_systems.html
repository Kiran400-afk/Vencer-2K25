Embedded Systems Challenge

Design Smart Embedded Solutions

Event Description

The Embedded Systems Challenge invites participants to develop innovative embedded solutions for modern applications. From microcontroller programming to real-time operating systems, showcase your expertise in creating efficient and reliable embedded systems for IoT, automotive, and industrial applications.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team participation (2-3 members)
  • Duration: 12 hours
  • Design and implement embedded systems
  • Hardware platforms: Arduino, Raspberry Pi, or STM32
  • Must include firmware development
  • Real-time constraints handling required
  • Power optimization implementation
  • Present working prototype with documentation

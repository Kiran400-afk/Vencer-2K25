Digital Electronics Challenge

Innovate with Digital Circuit Design

Event Description

The Digital Electronics Challenge invites participants to create innovative digital circuit designs and solutions. From combinational logic to sequential circuits, showcase your expertise in designing efficient and optimized digital systems for modern electronic applications.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Individual or team participation (max 2 members)
  • Duration: 8 hours
  • Design and simulate digital circuits
  • Software allowed: Quartus Prime, Vivado, or ModelSim
  • Must include timing diagrams and simulations
  • Power and resource optimization required
  • FPGA implementation preferred
  • Present complete design documentation
